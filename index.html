About the Event

Fostering innovation and communication skills among the builders of tomorrow.

The Purpose

To provide a platform for students to share their innovative ideas, research, and perspectives on emerging technologies and global challenges.

Rules

  • Minimum 1 member per team.
  • Maximum 5 members per team.
  • Presentation time: 8 mins + 2 mins Q&A.
  • Judging criteria: Content, Delivery, Innovation.

Competition Topics

Choose a theme that sparks your interest and challenge your intellect.

Emerging Research in AI

Dive deep into the latest advancements, from Generative AI and LLMs to ethical AI and its impact on the global landscape.

Presentation Format

Guideline for preparing your 'Current Trends in AI' presentation.

Submission Guidelines

  • Format: PPTX or PDF only.
  • Slides: 10 to 15 slides total.
  • Font: Professional and readable (e.g., Arial, Calibri).
Download PPT Template

Recommended Structure

1 Title & Team Details
2 Abstract / Introduction
3 Problem Statement
4 Current AI Trends & Research
5 Use Cases & Applications
6 Ethical Considerations
7 Future Scope & Conclusion
8 References
